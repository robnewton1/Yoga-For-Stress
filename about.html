About Think Less and De-Stress(TLDS)

History

This organization was founded in the Philadelphia, Pa during the period in history known as the "The work stress war." Three classmates Teodora, Pedro and Robert joined together to form an aliance to fight against the ever expanding stress forces. Together they found that yoga was the only way to make work envoirnments eco-friendly again.

Success!

Since the movement began in September 20, 2018 this organization reached over 1 million people struggling in the work place with stress. We've been listed as a top priority with in many companies.
