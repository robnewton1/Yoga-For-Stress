How do I become a volunteer?

Hello! We're a glad you want to join the movement and help hundreds of people!

The first step is filling the Application with your personal information.

Second, you may upload a video of your practicing yoga and showing your skills. Someone in the staff will watch it and check if you meet the requirements to teach yoga.

Finally, now you only have to wait for our e-mail with further instructions.

Good luck!
